Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Magic Coloring World handles information when you use our Android app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • No account registration or login required.
  • No names, emails, phone numbers, or location data collected.
  • Artwork is saved locally on your device only.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Google Play Services may collect anonymous usage data (device type, OS version, crash reports) to improve app stability. This cannot identify individual users.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children aged 3 and above under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us to request removal of any such data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (if ads are shown)

5. Data Storage

All artwork and preferences are stored locally. No user content is uploaded to any server.

6. Permissions

  • Storage: To save and load coloring artwork.
  • Internet: Used by Google Play Services and optional ads.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
